Problem Statement

Online job scams are evolving faster than traditional manual review processes.

Rising Threat of Fake Job Postings

Every year, thousands of job seekers fall victim to fraudulent job postings that promise unrealistic salaries, request upfront payments, or harvest sensitive personal information. These scams are often professional-looking, highly targeted, and extremely difficult to distinguish from legitimate opportunities.

Traditional job boards rely heavily on manual moderation and user reports, which do not scale to the volume and speed of modern phishing and scam campaigns. Job seekers need automated, intelligent protection that can analyze job content in real time and surface hidden red flags before damage occurs.

AI‑Driven Solution

An intelligent platform that reads and understands job descriptions like a security expert.

🤖

AI Classification

Fine‑tuned RoBERTa model trained on real and fake job datasets to predict whether a job posting is legitimate or fraudulent.

🔍

Suspicious Keyword Detection

NLP-based keyword and phrase analysis detects scam indicators such as advance fees, unrealistic earnings, and urgent hiring language.

💸

Salary Anomaly Detection

Rule‑assisted model flags unusually high compensation structures and inconsistent salary patterns that often accompany fake postings.

📧

Recruiter Email Verification

Checks whether recruiter emails use trusted corporate domains instead of free or disposable email providers commonly used in scams.
